Sushi

Fish, vegetables, rice and seaweed

Well it is basically something eatable mixed with rice and seaweed. It's FUCKING DELICIOUS

Ingredients

  • freesh fish
  • seaweed sheets
  • sushi rice
  • vegetables

Steps

  1. Cook the sushi rice
  2. Cover the sheet of seaweed with an even layer of prepared sushi rice
  3. Layer the freesh fish or the vegetables on the rice and roll it up tightly
  4. Slice with a sharp knife
