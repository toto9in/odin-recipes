Brigadeiro

A delicious braziliam's sweet recipe

Just a few ingredients and you can make one of the favorites food by the brazilians

Ingredients

  • sweetened condensed milk
  • cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • chocolate spinkles

Steps

  1. In a pot over low heat, melt the butter, condensed milk, and cocoa powder, stirring continuosly until you can see the bottom of the pot for 2-3 seconds when dragging a spatula through
  2. Pour onto a greased plate, then chill for 1 hour
  3. Shape and roll the cilled mixture into balls
  4. Roll the balls in chocolate spinkles
